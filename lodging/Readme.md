### General vacation rentals and homestays

- Airbnb: A large and popular platform with a wide variety of options, including rooms in people's homes and entire properties.
- Vrbo: Another major site for vacation rentals, often focused on whole homes, though it also has shared rooms.
- Booking.com: While known for hotels, it also includes a large inventory of alternative accommodations like apartments and holiday homes.
- HomeToGo: A metasearch engine that pulls listings from many different providers, including vacation rental sites.
- Sonder: Focuses on urban apartments, but can be a good option for city stays.

### Specific homestay and cultural immersion sites

- Bedycasa: Connects travelers with locals and provides a wide range of homestay-type options, including farms and riads.
- Culture Go Go: A site specifically designed to connect people who want to travel with locals who want to practice their English.
- Homestayweb.com: Appears to be a direct competitor in the homestay space, according to Similarweb.
- Homestayin.com: Another site that focuses on homestays and has appeared on competitor lists.

### Other related alternatives

- Hipcamp: If your interest is in staying outdoors, Hipcamp connects you with camping and outdoor experiences on private land.
- Couchsurfing: Connects travelers with hosts for free stays, with a focus on the social and cultural exchange aspect.
- Trusted Housesitters: A platform for house and pet sitting, which can be a way to stay in someone's home for free in exchange for caretaking.
